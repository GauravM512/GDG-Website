What Is GDG on Campus?

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are communities where developers and technologists connect to learn, share knowledge, and grow with Google's technologies. These groups foster innovation and collaboration in the tech world, often focusing on specific fields or applications of technology.

GDGs on Campus are tailored for university and college students, providing a platform to gain practical experience, develop technical and soft skills, and prepare for a tech-driven career. These campus-based groups organize events, coding sessions, and mentorship programs to empower aspiring developers. Upon graduation, members can join broader GDG communities to continue their learning journey and connect with a global network of developers.
